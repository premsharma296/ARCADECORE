NEON ARCHITECTURAL PROTOCOLS

1. HOW TO BUILD (VECTOR DRAWING)

Select an Intent Category on the left menu (Logistics, Utilities, Industry, or Combat). Then, click and drag your cursor on the 3D grid to trace energy walls.

2. AI SHAPE INTERPRETATION RULES

  • Open Lines (Straight/Curved): Translates to lines of transportation (Roads, Power Lines, or Laser Defense Barriers).
  • Closed Loops (Boxes/Circles): Tracing back to the start coordinate triggers production hubs: Generators (under utilities), Factories / Markets (under industry), or Shield Domes (under combat).

3. ECO LOGISTICS & COMBAT

Drones physically transport resources (Energy/Crystals) along roads. Protect your lines! Opponent structures can be **hacked** in real-time, and lightning/solar storms can short-circuit unprotected grids.
